About Me

I have over 43 years experience in project management, design engineering, process designs and mechanical/piping detailed engineering of a variety of process plants, including chemical and petrochemical, oil and gas processing, biofuels, pharmaceutical and food processing, pulp and paper, mining and mineral processing and environmental control projects. I have extensive experience in front end engineering design, and feasibility, process and HAZOP studies.. I have also been responsible for the performance of projects managed and have been responsible for preparing sectoral strategic plans, business development, international procurement and contract negotiations.

Welcome to my website. I am happy to have you here.  Please connect with me via LinkedIn, e-mail, or Zoom video if you wish to discuss anything or require any consulting.
